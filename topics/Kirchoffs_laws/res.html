Kirchhoff's laws

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
  (Redirected from Kirchoffs Laws)
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Not to be confused with Kerckhoffs's principle.

There are several Kirchhoff's laws, all named after Gustav Kirchhoff:

  • Kirchhoff's circuit laws in electrical engineering
  • Kirchhoff's law of thermal radiation
  • Kirchhoff equations in fluid dynamics
  • Kirchhoff's three laws of spectroscopy
  • Kirchhoff's law of thermochemistry
